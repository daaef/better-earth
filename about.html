WHO We Are?

The BETTER EARTH FOUNDATION is a non-profit organisation, aimed at raising awareness about the need to conserve nature and the environment and taking positive actions to combat environmental problems. Our work falls into four broad areas; Environmental conservation education, school outreach programmes, climate change mitigation and Public engagement on environmental issues.

She was founded on the 3rd of February, 2017. It started off as a pet project in 2017 and later upgraded to a non-profit in 2019. Our Head office is located in Jos, Nigeria. So far, we have been able to reach 1936 school children through our school outreach program and also set up environmental conservation clubs in these schools. Thanks to our numerous volunteers. This is our official website where we share our activities.

You can visit our website to find out more about our work and how you can get involved.
