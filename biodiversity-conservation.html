Biodiversity Conservation

Awareness for Biodiversity Conservation

We are in a biodiversity crisis as the world is headed for a sixth mass extinction as a result of human activities. However, there is still a huge gap between people and their awareness about nature conservation, particularly In Africa and we hope to fill this gap.

Through social media and public enlightenments campaigns and engagements, we hope to reach out to as many as we can on the stories, threats our biodiversity is faced with, how these threats affect us and how we can help.
